FLOWCHART FOR LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

[Start] | v [Display Menu] | v [Read Choice] | +—-(1)—-> [Add Book] —->
[Return to Menu] | +—-(2)—-> [Add Member] –> [Return to Menu] |
+—-(3)—-> [Search Book] -> [Return to Menu] | +—-(4)—-> [Issue Book] –>
[Return to Menu] | +—-(5)—-> [Return Book] -> [Return to Menu] |
+—-(6)—-> [Display Books] -> [Return to Menu] | +—-(7)—-> [Display
Members] -> [Return to Menu] | +—-(8)—-> [Exit] | v [End]

DESCRIPTION: - Program shows a menu with options 1–8. - User enters a
choice. - Each option calls a specific function. - After completing the
action, control returns to the menu. - Option 8 terminates the program.
